## SEO Best Practices Applied

### Keyword Placement Strategy
```
Location          | Keyword            | Priority
-----------------|-------------------|----------
H1 / Hero Text    | Primary Keywords  | High
Headings (H2/H3)  | Secondary Keywords| High
First 100 words   | Main Keyword      | High
Alt Text (Images) | Long-tail Keywords| Medium
URLs              | Primary Keyword   | High
Meta Description  | Primary Keyword   | High
Body Copy         | Mix of all tiers  | Medium
```

### Content Structure
- **Hero Section**: Keyword-rich headline + immediate value proposition
- **Certifications**: Trust-building with searchable credentials
- **Services Grid**: Comprehensive service list (static layout) for E-A-T signals
- **Team Section**: Professional profiles improve E-A-T score
- **Sustainability**: Aligns with modern user search intent
- **FAQs & Blog**: Long-form content targets long-tail keywords
